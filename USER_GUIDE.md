# Hospital AI Assistant - User Guide

## For Patients

### Getting Started

1. **Access the Platform**
   - Visit the Hospital AI Assistant website
   - Click "Sign In" to authenticate with your hospital credentials
   - First-time users will be guided through account setup

2. **Understanding the Medical Disclaimer**
   - The AI assistant provides clinical decision support only
   - It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice
   - Always consult with a licensed healthcare provider
   - In case of emergency, call 911 immediately

### Symptom Assessment

#### Step 1: Describe Your Symptoms

1. Click "Start Symptom Assessment" on the home page
2. Enter each symptom you're experiencing:
   - **Symptom Name**: Be specific (e.g., "chest pain," "persistent cough")
   - **Duration**: How long you've had the symptom (e.g., "2 days," "1 week")
   - **Severity**: Rate from 1-10 (1 = mild, 10 = severe)
3. Click "Add Symptom" to add more symptoms
4. Click "Next" when finished

#### Step 2: Medical History

Provide important health context:

1. **Allergies**
   - List any known drug allergies (e.g., "Penicillin")
   - Include food allergies if relevant to medications
   - Click "Add" to add each allergy

2. **Chronic Conditions**
   - List ongoing health conditions (e.g., "Diabetes," "Hypertension")
   - Include past surgeries if relevant
   - Click "Add" to add each condition

3. **Current Medications**
   - Enter each medication you're taking
   - Include dosage (e.g., "500mg")
   - Include frequency (e.g., "twice daily")
   - Click "Add Medication" for each one

4. **Family History** (Optional)
   - Note any relevant family medical history
   - Include conditions that run in your family

#### Step 3: Review & Submit

1. Review all information you've entered
2. Make corrections if needed using the "Back" button
3. Click "Submit for Analysis" to send to AI
4. Wait for the system to analyze your symptoms

### Viewing Results

After analysis completes:

1. **AI Analysis Results**
   - Possible conditions are listed with confidence levels
   - Urgency level indicates how soon you should seek care
   - Recommendations provide general guidance

2. **Important Notes**
   - All results include medical disclaimers
   - Results are for informational purposes only
   - Schedule an appointment with your doctor
   - Seek immediate care if symptoms worsen

3. **Your Interaction History**
   - Access past assessments from your dashboard
   - Track symptom changes over time
   - Share results with your healthcare provider

### Managing Your Documents

1. **Upload Medical Documents**
   - Click "Upload Document" in your dashboard
   - Select document type (medical record, image, lab result, etc.)
   - Choose file from your computer
   - Document is securely encrypted and stored

2. **Access Your Documents**
   - View all uploaded documents in your dashboard
   - Documents are only visible to you and authorized medical staff
   - Download documents as needed

### Privacy & Security

- **Your Data is Protected**
  - All information is encrypted
  - Access is restricted to authorized medical staff
  - Your data is never sold or shared
  - Compliant with healthcare privacy laws

- **What We Collect**
  - Symptoms and medical history you provide
  - Medications and allergies
  - Medical documents you upload
  - System access logs for security

- **Your Rights**
  - Request access to your data
  - Request corrections to your information
  - Request deletion of your account
  - File privacy complaints with hospital administration

## For Medical Staff (Doctors & Nurses)

### Accessing the Clinical Dashboard

1. **Login**
   - Use your hospital credentials
   - Admin role required for dashboard access
   - Session expires after 30 minutes of inactivity

2. **Dashboard Overview**
   - Pending interactions requiring review
   - System alerts and urgent notifications
   - Audit logs and compliance reports
   - Patient interaction history

### Reviewing Patient Interactions

#### Pending Interactions

1. **View Flagged Cases**
   - Click "Pending Interactions" in dashboard
   - Cases are sorted by urgency level
   - Red indicators show critical urgency

2. **Review Interaction Details**
   - Click on a patient interaction
   - View patient's symptoms and medical history
   - Review AI-suggested conditions
   - Check medication recommendations

3. **AI Analysis Information**
   - **Possible Conditions**: Listed with confidence scores
   - **Urgency Level**: Routine, Moderate, Urgent, or Critical
   - **Recommendations**: General clinical suggestions
   - **Drug Interactions**: Medication safety alerts

#### Approving or Escalating

1. **Review Staff Form**
   - Enter your clinical notes
   - Document your assessment
   - Indicate any additional tests needed

2. **Make Decision**
   - **Approve**: Accept AI recommendation and mark resolved
   - **Escalate**: Flag for specialist review or further investigation
   - **Override**: Provide alternative clinical guidance

3. **Submit Review**
   - Click "Submit Review"
   - Interaction is logged with your notes
   - Patient receives notification if applicable

### Drug Interaction Checking

1. **Manual Medication Check**
   - Enter medication names
   - List patient allergies
   - System checks for interactions
   - Review warnings and contraindications

2. **Interpreting Results**
   - **Green (Safe)**: No known interactions
   - **Yellow (Warning)**: Minor interactions, monitor patient
   - **Red (Critical)**: Serious interactions, avoid combination

### Managing System Alerts

1. **Alert Types**
   - **Urgent Symptom**: Patient reported critical symptoms
   - **Compliance Event**: System access or data modification
   - **System Alert**: Technical or operational issues

2. **Responding to Alerts**
   - Click alert to view details
   - Take appropriate action (contact patient, escalate, etc.)
   - Click "Acknowledge" when handled
   - Acknowledgment is logged for compliance

### Viewing Audit Logs

1. **Access Audit Trail**
   - Click "Audit Logs" in clinical dashboard
   - View all system activities
   - Filter by user, date, or action type

2. **Log Information**
   - User who accessed data
   - Action performed (view, edit, upload, etc.)
   - Timestamp and IP address
   - Resource accessed
   - Compliance event flag

3. **Using for Compliance**
   - Verify authorized access only
   - Identify suspicious activities
   - Generate compliance reports
   - Support regulatory audits

### Patient Interaction Summary

For each patient, review:

1. **Medical History**
   - Current medications
   - Known allergies
   - Chronic conditions
   - Surgical history

2. **Interaction Timeline**
   - Previous assessments
   - AI recommendations
   - Staff reviews and decisions
   - Follow-up actions

3. **Documents**
   - Medical records uploaded
   - Lab results
   - Imaging studies
   - Prescription history

### Best Practices

#### Clinical Decision Making

- **Use AI as a Tool**: AI analysis supports, not replaces, clinical judgment
- **Consider Context**: Patient history and presentation matter
- **Verify Information**: Double-check patient allergies and medications
- **Document Decisions**: Always record your clinical reasoning
- **Escalate When Needed**: Refer to specialists for complex cases

#### Security & Compliance

- **Protect Patient Privacy**: Never share patient data outside authorized channels
- **Secure Logout**: Always log out before leaving your workstation
- **Report Issues**: Notify IT of any security concerns immediately
- **Follow Protocols**: Adhere to hospital policies and procedures
- **Audit Regularly**: Review access logs for unauthorized activity

#### Patient Communication

- **Explain Results**: Help patients understand AI findings
- **Provide Context**: Discuss limitations of AI analysis
- **Recommend Follow-up**: Schedule appropriate appointments
- **Answer Questions**: Address patient concerns about privacy and accuracy
- **Document Conversations**: Record patient interactions in medical record

## Troubleshooting

### Common Patient Issues

**Q: I forgot my password**
- Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Follow email instructions to reset
- Contact hospital IT if email not received

**Q: My symptom assessment isn't submitting**
- Ensure you've added at least one symptom
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if problem persists

**Q: I can't upload a document**
- Verify file size is under 50MB
- Check file format is supported (PDF, JPG, PNG)
- Ensure you have storage space
- Try again or contact support

**Q: How long does AI analysis take?**
- Analysis typically completes in 30-60 seconds
- Longer waits may indicate system load
- Results are saved in your history

### Common Staff Issues

**Q: I can't access the clinical dashboard**
- Verify you have admin role
- Check that you're logged in
- Try clearing browser cache
- Contact IT to verify permissions

**Q: Audit logs not showing my action**
- Logs update every 5 minutes
- Refresh page to see latest entries
- Check date range filter
- Contact IT if logs are missing

**Q: Patient interaction won't load**
- Verify patient ID is correct
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Contact IT support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Patients

**Q: Is my information private?**
A: Yes. Your data is encrypted, protected by security controls, and only accessible to authorized medical staff. We comply with healthcare privacy laws.

**Q: Can I trust the AI analysis?**
A: The AI provides clinical decision support based on evidence-based guidelines. However, it's not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult with your doctor.

**Q: What happens if I report critical symptoms?**
A: Critical symptoms are immediately flagged and escalated to medical staff. You may receive a call from the hospital to follow up.

**Q: Can I share my results with my doctor?**
A: Yes. You can download your results and share them with any healthcare provider.

### For Medical Staff

**Q: How accurate is the AI analysis?**
A: The AI is trained on evidence-based medical guidelines and provides reasonable suggestions. However, clinical judgment and patient context are essential for final diagnosis.

**Q: What if I disagree with the AI recommendation?**
A: You can override AI recommendations with your clinical assessment. Document your reasoning in the review form.

**Q: How are patient privacy concerns handled?**
A: All access is logged, encrypted, and audited. Unauthorized access is detected and reported. Follow hospital policies for data handling.

**Q: Can I export patient data?**
A: Patient data can be accessed through the dashboard. For bulk exports, contact your system administrator.
